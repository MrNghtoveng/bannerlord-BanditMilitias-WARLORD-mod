AI Architecture

Bandit Militias: WARLORD Edition does not use one single AI model.
It combines:

- strategic reasoning
- tactical decision rules
- scheduler-driven execution
- telemetry and learning support
- world-context systems
- patch-based integration

MAIN SYSTEMS

- BanditBrain
- WarlordSystem
- HTNEngine
- MilitiaDecider
- SwarmCoordinator
- AdaptiveAIDoctrineSystem
- AILearningSystem
- PlayerTracker
- FearSystem
- SpatialGridSystem
- PartyCleanupSystem
- Harmony patches

In this project, AI means the combined behavior of rules, scheduling, context tracking, telemetry, and world simulation feedback.
