AI Architecture

Bandit Militias: WARLORD Edition does not use one single AI model.
It combines:

- strategic reasoning
- tactical decision rules
- scheduler-driven execution
- telemetry and learning support
- world-context systems
- patch-based integration

MAIN SYSTEMS

- BanditBrain
- WarlordSystem
- HTNEngine
- WarlordTacticalMissionBehavior
- MilitiaDecider
- SwarmCoordinator
- AdaptiveAIDoctrineSystem
- AILearningSystem
- PlayerTracker
- FearSystem
- SpatialGridSystem
- PartyCleanupSystem
- Harmony patches

In this project, AI means the combined behavior of rules, scheduling, context tracking, telemetry, and world simulation feedback.

NOTES

- HTNEngine is the campaign-side execution bridge used by the bandit AI patch.
- WarlordTacticalMissionBehavior is the mission-time tactical planner that turns doctrine choice into live HTN plans.
- Primitive HTN task preconditions are now active, so doctrine plans only execute tasks that still fit the current world state.
- The internal EventBus is pumped from SubModule.OnApplicationTick and becomes fully active after deferred bootstrap completes.
